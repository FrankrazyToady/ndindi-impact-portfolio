Samson Ndindi Nyoro

Visionary Leader | MP Kiharu | Entrepreneur | Economist

Profile Overview

Ndindi Nyoro is a visionary Kenyan leader, entrepreneur, and economist. He has transformed education in Kiharu, empowered youth, and championed fiscal discipline and anti-corruption reforms. He represents a new generation of principled, results-oriented leadership.

Professional & Business Achievements

  • Built successful businesses in investment, telecommunications, and financial markets.
  • Co-founded Afrisec Telecoms and Stockbridge Securities.
  • Founded Investax Capital, promoting financial literacy and investment culture.
  • Provides mentorship for young entrepreneurs across Kenya.
  • Recognized for practical solutions combining business acumen and community development.

Political Leadership & Contributions

  • Member of Parliament for Kiharu Constituency (2017–present)
  • Former Chairperson, Budget & Appropriations Committee
  • Oversaw national budget allocations and promoted development priorities
  • Sponsored Anti-Corruption and Economic Crimes legislation
  • Promotes data-driven, transparent governance
  • Engages in national policy debates on economics and youth empowerment
